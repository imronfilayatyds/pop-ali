POP IT!

The best anti-stress toy - endless bubble wrap!

What is Pop It?

Pop It literally translates as "pop it". It's a children's game, the process of which can be compared to crumpling bubble wrap. But unlike bubble wrap, Pop It can be pressed with characteristic sensations and sounds "infinitely": dozens of silicone bubbles are simply squeezed to the other side.

Game rules

Players take turns pressing the bubbles. You must always finish the row you started. You can press a maximum of 3 bubbles at a time (they must be next to each other, you can't press several bubbles at once if they are not adjacent! The goal is to make the opponent move last. Whoever moves last loses.
